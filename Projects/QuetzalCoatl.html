QuetzalCoatl

3d physics based snake game with adventure and dungeon crawler elements

About QuetzalCoatl

Quetzalcoatl is a dungeon adventure puzzle game i made with 2 other developers and 4 artists in 2020 as a school assigment for my exams call MYTH, in the game the player takes control of QuetzalCoatl, a mythical god created by the historical native tribes of the Mayans and Aztec. The game takes place in 2 areas, a maze and a temple. the player has to do puzzles, maneuver as a snake and dodge and escape traps.

Programs and languages used

  • Unity Engine
  • Unity C#
  • Photoshop
  • AutoDesk Maya
